Tribolium castaneum

Colour key for the taxonomic levels shown in the diagram above.

Both CSFB and the Red Flour Beetle share the same order, Coleoptera (beetles). Tribolium castaneum is a classic and widely used model organism for Coleoptera research — its short generation time, ease of laboratory culture, and well-characterised genome make it the go-to system for investigating beetle biology, including the gene function relevant to understanding CSFB.

Adults

  • Colour

    Shiny, reddish-brown or rust coloured

  • Size

    Small, 3–4.5 mm; flat-bodied

  • Features

    Able to fly

Larvae

  • Colour

    Creamy yellow to light brown with a darker head

  • Size

    Slender, cylindrical; up to 6–7 mm long
